Q.16. Ïa ki jait jingrwai aïu la ñiew kum ki symbai
ka jingrwai sonnet?
Jubab: Ía ki jingrwai ba ki khot strambotto.

Q.17. Kaei ka strambotto?
Jubab: Ki dei ki jait jingrwai kiba kynthup ar tylli
ki quatrain bad bud da ka refrain kaba hynriew laiñ.
