Jubab: Ka bud ïa kane ka rukom hap kyntien abcb.

Q.06. Ka poem “Ka Shnong Jingthala” ka dei ka
jait poem aïu?
Jubab: Ka dei ka ballad.

Q.07. Kumno ngi tip ba ka poem “Ka Shnong
Jingthala” ka dei ka ballad?
Jubab: Ngi tip ba ka dei ka ballad namar:-

- Ka don saw laiñ shi dkhot, la thoh ha ka dur
ka jingiathuh khana, ki laiñ 1 bad 3 ki bud ïa ka
tetrameter (phra kynnoh shi laiñ), ki laiñ 2 bad 4 ki
bud ïa ka frimeter (hynriew kynnoh shi laiñ) bad
ka rukom hap kyntien ka long abcb (ka laiñ kaba
ar ka hap kyntien bad ka laiñ kaba saw).

Q.08. Kaei ka Bawsis?

Jubab: Ka dei ka puron kaba ngi shem ha ka poem
“Ka Shnong Jingthala”, ka dei ka tnga jong u
Philemon, ki shong ki sah shabar na ka shnong
jingthala, bad kim don khun don kti.

Q.09. Ki jait briew aïu ki shong shnong ha ka
Shnong Jingthala?

Jubab: Ki dei ki briew kiba sangrang ka dohnud,
ki bym ju pynmih ‘tien ieit ‘tien sbun, kiba nang
ban sei tang ki kyntien ka hangamei bad ‘tien
amrang, ki ‘tien sat ‘tien jew, kiba mlien ka peit
mat kynriang, ka ktah matïong bad kiba leh ki kam
kynsha.
