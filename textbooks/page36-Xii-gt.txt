Q.15. Kaei ka Adjective?

Jubab: Ka dei ka klas kyntien kaba pyndep ïa kine
ki kam:

- Ka pynshai īa ka noun.

- Ka ñiew ïa ka jingdon jong ka noun ne kyrdan ba

- ka kdew ïa ka jinglong ka noun.

- Ka ïanujor īa ka noun bad kawei ne kiwei pat ki

Q.16. Ai nuksa ïa ka noun kaba long adjective?
Jubab: ïüngjaiñ, lingmaw, miettlang, mietlyiur. (jaiñ,
maw, tlang, iyiur ki dei ki noun lada shim tang
marwei hynrei hangne ki dei ki adjective).

Q.17. Ai nuksa īa ka adjective kiba long noun?
Jubab: Ha kane ka juk u baduk u thiah suk
mynmiet.(baduk ka dei ka adjective hynrei hangne
ka long noun).
