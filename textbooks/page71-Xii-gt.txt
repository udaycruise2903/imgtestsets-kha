(g) Ka Aiom Renaissance.

Jubab: Ka aïom Renaissance, ka dei ka aïom ka
thoh ka pule ha ri England bad kiwei ki bynta jong
ka ri Europe ha kaba ka thoh ka pule ka ïaid ryngkat
bad kiwei ki tnat jingpule. Dei ha kane ka aiom ba
ka jingdro dur, ki jingpule philosophy bad kiwei ki
tnat jong ka pule ka puthi ki ïatrei ryngkat bad
pynmih bad pynkha ki jingthoh kiba sngewtynnat.
Kane ka aïom renaissance ka la pynmih shibun
ngut ki ņnongthoh kiba khraw ha ka pyrthei.
