Q.07. Kaei ka sonnet?

Jubab: Ka dei ka lyric poetry, kaba ka don ka dur
ka dar kaba thikna, kata 14 laiñ, 10 kynnoh ha
kawei pa kawei ka laiñ bad ruh ka jiņngthew tara
kaba la buh pyrkhing.

Q.08. Mano ba ong, “Ngan shah ïa lade ban khot ïa
kine, ki sonnet kiba kynthup khatsaw laiñ, bad man
kawei pa kawei ka laiñ ka don shiphew kynnoh.”
Jubab: U George Gascoigne.

Q.09. Ha ka spah snem kaba katno bad na kano
ka jaka ka sonnet ka la sdang ban thylleng?
Jubab: Ha ka spah snem kaba khatlai, na Sicily.

Q.10. Na Sicily ka sonnet ka la suh thied shano?
Jubab: Sha Tuscany.

Q.11. Ha ka spah snem kaba katno bad ha ki kti
jongno ka sonnet ka la sdang ban tynshaiñ phyrnai?
Jubab: Ha ka spah snem kaba khatsaw, ha ki kti u
Petrarch.

Q.12. ĪÏano la ju ñiew kum u Kpa ka jingrwai
sonnet?
Jubab: Ïa u Francesco Petrarch.

Q.13. Uei u Francesco Petrarch?
Jubab: U myllung bakhraw ka ri Italy (1304-1374).

Q.14. Kat kum u Theodore Watts Dunton uei u
nongthoh sonnet banyngkong tam?
Jubab: U Fra Guittone d’ Arezzo.
