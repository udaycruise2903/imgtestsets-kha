“KA JINGSNGEWTYNNAT JONG KA JINGPULE-
KOT”

Q.01. Ka jingpule Kòt ka leh aïu ha u briew?

Jubab: Ka jingpule kot ka pynroi ïa ka jingnang
jingtip, ka pyniar ia ka rukom pyrkhat bad ka bor
pyrkhat jong u briew.

Q.02. U briew uba pule kot u long u briew uba kumno?
Jubab: U long u briew uba shem phang, uba sngewthuh
ïa ka rukom pyrkhat jong kiwei bad uba ïar ka jingmut
jingpyrkhat.

Q.03. Ïa u briew uba la heh ka rta hynrei uba dang
duna ha ka kyrdan pule ki Khasi ki ong kumno?
Jubab: “Ka rynieng kat u siej, ka jingmut pat dang biej.”

Q.04. Ïa u briew uba khim ka jingmut jingpyrkhat
la ïanujor bad no?

Jubab: La ïanujor bad u ‘sew-ap-īïing ha shiah ksew
uba kii īa lade.

Q.05. Ïa u briew uba minot ha ka jingpule bad uba
ngam jylliew ki Knasi ki khot kumno?
Jubab: U Liengjathang kynjih sha Mawsiang bneng.

Q.06. Khnang ba ka jabieng kan long kaba koit ba
khiah ka don kam ïa kaei?

Jubab: Ka don kam ïa ka jingmlien ban pule, namar
da kaba leh kumta ka ïoh ban trei kam.
