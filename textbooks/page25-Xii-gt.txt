Jubab: Ki Khasi-Jaiñtia ki long ki briew kiba smat
bad ba minot shibun ha ka trei ka ktah. Kim ju riej
ban trei ïa kano kano ka kam. Kim ju leh raiñ ne
ñiew poh ïa kano kano ka jingtrei.

Q.02. Malu mala ha ki katno bynta lah ban
pynbynta ïa ka Ri Khasi-Jaiñtia? Kiei kita?
Jubab: Ha ki lai bynta. Ka bynta ba sharum, ka
thaiñ Ri-War; ka bynta ba hapdeng, ka thaiñ Ri-
Lum bad ka bynta ba shaneng, ka thaiñ Ri-Bhoi.

Q.03. Ïa kano ka thaiñ la ñiew kum ka kper Eden
jong ka Ri Khasi-Jaiñtia?
Jubab: Ïa ka thaiñ Ri-War.

Q.04. Ki jait syntiew aïu ngj ïoh na ka thaiñ Ri-War?
Jubab: Ngi ioh ïa u ‘tiewlasubon, u ‘tiew-diengngai
bad u ‘tiew-shympa.

Q.O5. Ki jait soh aïu ngi ïoh na ki thaiñ Ri-War?
Jubab: Ki sohñiamtra, ki sohkwid, ki sohkynphor
bad ki sohtrun.

Q.06. Nalor ki bri soh, doh sa ki bri aïu ha Ri-War?
Jubab: Don ki bri tympew bad ki bri kwai.

Q.07. Na ki sla sohtrun ki ïoh aïu bad ki leh aïu ïa ki?
Jubab: Ki īïoh ki ksai kiba jwat bad ki thaiñ kynja
iarong ne kynja jar.

Q.08. Kumno ki Khasi ki pyndon kam īa ka
latyrpad?

Jubab: Kum ka kynja musla jyntah.
